nice to meet you.

meet tessa.

Tessa Smith is a writer and collage artist who uses the keyboard to express the music she hears inside her head. Eric translates her visions into more complex and beautiful arrangements for Star Projector. Her influences include Lomelda, Florist and Field Medic. She spends her days writing, creating art and learning web design.

meet eric.

Eric Jaques is a guitarist, bassist, and vocalist who has learned how to record and mix music at home in the past few years. He also plays keyboard and drums for Star Projector. A few influences of his include The Beatles, Pinegrove and Alex G, but most of his free time is spent listening to podcasts and watching 80s horror films.
